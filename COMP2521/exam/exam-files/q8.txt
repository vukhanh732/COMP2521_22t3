Question 8

(a) What is the maximum number of words that could be contained in the
    trie? Justify your answer.
n number of words in the trie. There are n nodes, we can mark every nodes to be finishing nodes, therefore it can have a maximum n numbers of words.


(b) What is the minimum number of words that could be contained in the
    trie? Justify your answer.
m number of words. There are m leaf nodes, each leaf node is a finishing node, therefore it would have minimum m number of words in the tree.
